## Important Notes for Students

- Submit your repository URL **once** at the beginning of the semester
- All labs are graded by GitHub Actions status (Weeks 3-14)
- You can resubmit unlimited times before the deadline
- The final GitHub Actions status at the deadline determines your grade
- Green checkmark ✅ = full credit, Red X ❌ = no credit
